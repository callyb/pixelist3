Links

Useful Links for mums to be and new mums


Antenatal

NHS Choices is a evidenced based website to access information around pregnancy and babycare

http://www.nhs.uk/Conditions/pregnancy-and-baby/pages/pregnancy-and-baby-care.aspx"

Best Beginnings is a charitable organisation that works with the Department of Health and has created an evidence based webpage and app called Babybuddy which is for pregnant women and new families. Best Beginnings is also creating a similar page for women suffering from maternal mental health concerns.

https://www.bestbeginnings.org.uk/about-baby-buddy

Baby Sense - the advice and tips section is worth a look

http://www.babysense.com/category/advice-and-tips/pregnancy-and-birth-talk/

Department of Health 'Off to the best start' booklet is free to download and is a resource for women who are considering breastfeeding as their feeding choice.

http://www.unicef.org.uk/Documents/Baby_Friendly/Leaflets/otbs_leaflet.pdf

Postnatal

Understanding childhood has lots of downloadable leaflets to support parents from the early days through to teenage years helping to link baby brain development and emotional development with advice and tips.

http://www.understandingchildhood.net/

Baby Sense – the advice and tips section is worth a look

http://www.babysense.com/category/advice-and-tips/baby/baby-talk

Feeding

Department of Health Guide to introducing solid foods is downloadable

https://www.gov.uk/government/uploads/system/uploads/attachment_data/file/216328/dh_125828.pdf

Breastfeeding Network has lots of publications and leaflets to download such as over the counter medications and breastfeeding and expressing and storage.

https://www.breastfeedingnetwork.org.uk/

Miscellaneous

I love this website. It is home grown in Durham and offers lots of evidence based information for parents around sleep, infants, breastfeeding, SIDS.

https://www.isisonline.org.uk/

A good website to read up on the latest evidence to promote, support and sustain breastfeeding in the UK.

http://www.unicef.org.uk/BabyFriendly/
